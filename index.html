Welcome to Divine Grace Unec

Motto: I can do all things through Christ that strengthens me

We are delighted to have you visit our online home, a space where faith, fellowship, and love converge. As part of the Divine Grace family, our mission is to glorify God, share His love, and foster a spirit of unity among all who seek Him. At Divine Grace UNEC, we are more than just a church; we are a community bound by our faith in Christ and a shared commitment to live out His teachings. Whether you are a first-time visitor, a returning friend, or a long-time member of our congregation, we extend to you a warm and heartfelt welcome.

WHO WE ARE

Divine Grace UNEC is a Christ-centered church situated in the heart of UNEC. We are a family of believers dedicated to worshiping God in spirit and truth, growing in His Word, and impacting our community with the Gospel. Our doors are always open to anyone seeking hope, healing, and a deeper connection with God.

OUR VALUE…

Divine Grace UNEC Campus is Known for…

Soul Winning

Here, soul winning is a command, and the very essence of our existence… Daily, with fervent zeal and dedication, we embark on the ministry of reconciling lost souls to the Father, through the expression of love and grace!

Vision

Develop and deploy transformational leaders as agents of change to the world.

Prayer

Prayer is our way of connecting with God, expressing worship, gratitude, and seeking His guidance.It invites His presence and power into our lives. In Divine Grace Unec, we breath prayer for the bible says "Do not be anxious about anything, but in every situation, by prayer and petition, with thanksgiving, present your requests to God." — Philippians 4:6

Rightfully dividing the word of God

We rightly divide the Word of God, interpret and apply the Scriptures with care and accuracy, ensuring God's truth is shared as He intended. "Be diligent to present yourself approved to God, a worker who does not need to be ashamed, rightly dividing the word of truth." — 2 Timothy 2:15
